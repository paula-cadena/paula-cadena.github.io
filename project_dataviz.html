Migration in Motion A Visual Exploration of Global Trends

Country Region

This interactive visualization project provides a comprehensive look at global migration trends using data from the UN International Migrant Stock and UN Population Projections. It explores migration patterns, demographic details, and regional trends across decades, offering a unique lens into how migration shapes societies worldwide.

You can navigate to: Country, and examine migration flows for specific countries, with interactive maps and charts displaying origin-destination connections and demographic breakdowns. or Region, and discover regional trends through net migration rates and subregional dynamics.

Data Sources:

United Nations, Population Division (2020) - International Migrant Stock and United Nations, Department of Economic and Social Affairs, Population Division (2024) - World Population Prospects

Migration in Motion A Visual Exploration of Global Trends

This page features an interactive map showcasing migration flows to and from individual countries, using cumulative data in five-year intervals starting from 1990. Explore: Connections to visualize the origin and destination flows, with the selected country as the focal point and Top Origins to display the top 5 countries of origin for migrants to the selected country.

Select a year and hover over a country on the map to update the visuals dynamically and dive deeper into migration insights.
